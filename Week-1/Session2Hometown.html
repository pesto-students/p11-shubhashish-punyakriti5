Ranchi, Jharkhand

Location

Ranchi is located in the southern part of the Chota Nagpur plateau, which is the eastern section of the Deccan plateau.

About

Weather

Ranchi has a humid subtropical climate. However, due to its position and the forests around the city, it is known for its pleasant climate.

Population

The current estimate population of Ranchi city in 2023 is 1,479,000 , while Ranchi metro population is estimated at 1,552,000 .

Sports

Cricket, Hockey and football are popular games with the people of Jharkhand. "M S Dhoni"-the legend of cricket is from this town only.

Things to do

  • Sightseeing at Patratu valley
  • Enjoy weekend in several waterfalls
  • Visit Jagannath and Rajrappa temple
